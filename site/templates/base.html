What is this site

This little platform allows you to write any movie review, may it be positive, negative, or something inbetween, and estimate it with the help of an almaighty AI! The model implemented here is quite simple but nonetheless rather competent, see for yourself! For detailed information feel free to consult the repository, it contains Jupyter notebooks and work reports, link is at the bottom.

How to use?

Simply write any review in the form on the right and submit! However, restrict yourself to English language and write in detail, the more the better. The model works relatively fast, due to its simplicity. This instruction will be replaced by a brief summary of how model made its decision.
